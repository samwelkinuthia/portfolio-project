E-mall

A platform where business owners can add their malls/businesses and their details, as well as manage the stores and spaces inside said businesses.

Initially started this project to enable frequent mallers ( people who visit malls..totally not a word i know.. ) to easily navigate malls through an online site.

Afterwards, it seemed logical to use the site to collect mall and store data and make it accessible to others via the E-mall api
